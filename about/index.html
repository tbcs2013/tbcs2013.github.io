Driving Tech Transformation with Passion

Think Big Computer Solutions (TBCS) — a proudly Malawian company built on passion, integrity, and innovation. Founded in 2008, our journey has been one of continuous growth, driven by a commitment to solving real-world challenges through technology.
Over the years, we’ve earned the trust of businesses, institutions, and communities by delivering reliable ICT and electronics solutions tailored to local needs. What started as a humble initiative has grown into a dynamic team of professionals who believe in thinking big, working smart, and serving with heart.

Behind the Screens: Our Story and Vision

The Think Big Journey

From our early days as CGM Software Professionals, to TERANCH Computer Solutions, and finally becoming Think Big in 2013 — our evolution is a reflection of resilience, passion, and purpose. Our name was inspired by the book “Think Big” by Dr. Ben Carson, a daily reminder that with big dreams and service-driven values, anything is possible.
